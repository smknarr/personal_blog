Project Title

Real Time Object Detection using Yolo v3 Architecture

Abstract

A security system that aims to provide a quick and easy solution to eliminate threat of burglars by notifying the owners about movements around their homes

Features

  • Real Time analysis
  • Sends a notification to the house owner as soon as a person is detected when owner isn't at home
  • Accurately identifies around 25 different objects
  • Scalability to other domains is very easy
