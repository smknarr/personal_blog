About me

The world has been technologically advancing at an exponential pace. With the advent of futuristic technologies, the realm of Computer Science has become the backbone of numerous research and developments. Having been born into a family of Computer Science researchers, I have always been a fanatic of understanding complex concepts in the field.

I earned my Bachelor of Engineering in Computer Science and Engineering at Ramaiah Institute of Technology, Bengaluru where I had the privilege of attending various classes that proved thoroughly influential not only with respect to academics but also with respect to my overall professional development. I was given several opportunities to develop copious projects that helped me apply the theory I was learning. This program not only broadened my perspective but also helped strengthen my personality and character.

The vast possibility of research, design and development has been my driving force to continue exploring comprehensive topics under Computer Science. During my time in my undergraduate program, I discovered my passion for Python programming, and was keen to dive into basics of Machine Learning and Data Science.

The theoretical assimilation of various subjects helped in the pragmatic application of the same in my corporate life.

I profoundly aspire to equip myself for the future which is why I am pursuing a graduate degree, and I firmly intend to pursue higher positions along with elaborate project roles in the tech industry. I aim to contribute my own unique perspectives to enhance and advance the field I am wholeheartedly passionate about and will unquestionably perdure to devote my career to Computer Science.
